I am a PhD researcher at **Delft University of Technology (TU Delft)** working on the **verification of learning-driven control in safety-critical systems**. I am part of the **Algorithmics group** in the **Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science (EEMCS)**.

I work in the **[SUMI Lab](https://annalukina.com/team/)** led by **Anna Lukina**, who is my PhD supervisor, and my promotor is **Matthijs T. J. Spaan**.

My research focuses on neural network-based controllers for dynamic and stochastic systems, with a particular interest in how formal guarantees can be maintained or reclaimed when systems change after verification. More broadly, I am interested in safe and reliable machine learning for control, certificate-based verification, and compositional methods.

You can find my publications and academic profiles through the links in the sidebar.

## Research interests

* Verification of sequential decision making
* Certificate-based verification
* Stochastic and uncertain dynamical systems
* Safety-critical autonomous systems
* Logic for safe AI
* Neural network controllers
* Compositional verification and control
